About Me

What I love most about data science? It can advance ANY topic. With a background spanning chemistry, physics, mathematics, and informatics, I've applied ML to everything from colorectal cancer and nutrition to astronomy, finance, and societal challenges. The interdisciplinary nature of this field is what drives me.

I get most energized in fast-paced, ambitious environments tackling novel, society-driven problems. I thrive on thinking big and coming up with creative solutions that make real impact. Currently, I'm deep into training transformers and researching how to use LLMs in data labeling while avoiding bias!

While I work independently, I love collaborating in small, focused teams. I'm not afraid of challenge; I aim high and embrace it! Fun fact: I have many parallel projects going at once. Even if I don't finish them all, they teach me something valuable, and that's what matters.
