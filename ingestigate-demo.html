Ingestigate

LLM-Driven Food Intelligence App

Project Overview

Ingestigate is an intelligent food-tracking application that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to help users understand and manage their nutrition in a privacy-conscious manner. Currently in private beta, the app represents a complete end-to-end solution built from concept to deployment.
