Ingestigate

LLM-Driven Food Intelligence App

Project Overview

Ingestigate is a comprehensive AI-powered nutrition tracking application that combines traditional food diary functionality with cutting-edge AI features to deliver truly smart nutrition insights. Built entirely from scratch in Flutter, it features a sophisticated health analytics system, personalized AI coaching, and innovative food discovery tools.

Six Health Anchors System

At the core of Ingestigate is a unique nutritional framework tracking six fundamental health anchors:

Protein

Adequate protein intake tracking

Fiber & Plant Diversity

Plant-based nutrition and fiber analysis

Fat Quality & Adequacy

Omega-3, saturated fat, and fat quality metrics

Micronutrients

Comprehensive vitamin and mineral tracking

Whole Foods Foundation

Processing level and food quality analysis

Gut Rest

Meal timing and digestive health

Core Features

Smart Food Logging

  • Multiple Input Methods: Manual search, barcode scanner, AI photo analysis, text-to-meal parsing, and recipe sharing via deep links
  • Intelligent Meal Creation: Create custom meals and recipes, share with other users, import shared meals
  • AI Smart Meal Generator: Creates complete meals based on your chosen cuisine, centered around specific foods and macros, while obeying all health pillars
  • Smart Serving Suggestions: AI-powered portion size recommendations

Coach

  • Intraday Feedback: Real-time guidance throughout the day based on your eating patterns
  • Meal-by-Meal Analysis: Instant personalized feedback on each logged meal
  • Daily & Weekly Summaries: Comprehensive nutrition reports and insights
  • Fully Personalized: All feedback respects your dietary preferences, allergies, and intolerances

Food Discovery Tools

  • Food Sage: Personalized food recommendations ranked by your current nutritional needs
  • Food Oracle: Advanced search filtering foods by multiple nutrients simultaneously
  • Dietary Filtering: Support for vegan, keto, paleo, and custom dietary preferences

Health Analytics & Reporting

  • Daily Scorecard: Performance tracking across all six health anchors
  • Weekly Trends: Visual charts and treemap visualization of progress
  • Intolerance Detection: AI-powered food-symptom pattern analysis

Technical Implementation

Technology Stack

Flutter 3.7.2+ Dart Supabase PostgreSQL OpenAI GPT TypeScript Edge Functions

Architecture Highlights

  • Frontend: Flutter cross-platform app (iOS, Android, Desktop) with Provider state management
  • Backend: Supabase with PostgreSQL database and real-time subscriptions
  • AI Integration: 9 custom Edge Functions for AI processing - meal parsing, photo analysis, coaching, intolerance detection
  • Data Management: Comprehensive food database with user-namespaced data, offline caching, and cloud sync
  • Privacy-First: Per-user data isolation, secure authentication, no cross-account data sharing

Key Services

  • DiaryService: Food logging and meal management
  • ScorecardService: Health anchor calculations and scoring algorithms
  • ChatService: AI coach conversation management
  • FoodOracleService: Intelligent food search and ranking
  • AntioxidantService: Phytonutrient and compound tracking

Impact & Vision

Ingestigate represents a new approach to nutrition tracking - one that goes beyond calorie counting to focus on holistic health through the Six Health Anchors framework. By combining AI intelligence with privacy-first design, the app makes sophisticated nutritional analysis accessible to everyone.

What Makes Ingestigate Different

  • Intelligence Without Friction: AI handles the complexity - users just snap photos or describe meals
  • Education Through Use: Every interaction teaches users about nutrition through personalized coaching
  • Privacy-First: All data is user-namespaced with no sharing between accounts or third parties
  • Holistic Health Focus: Six anchors framework addresses nutrition quality, not just quantity
  • Built for Real Life: Supports restaurant meals, home cooking, recipes, and everything in between

The app is currently in private beta with active users benefiting from personalized nutrition insights, AI coaching, and comprehensive health analytics.
